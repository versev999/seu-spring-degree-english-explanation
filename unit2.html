UNIT 2

研究计划书
Research Proposal

研究问题的好坏 · 人称代词回避 · 名词化 nominalization · 选题与拟题

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解研究计划书的重要性与主要组成部分；
  • 了解学术写作中人称代词的使用与名词化（nominalization）；
  • 学会启动研究并撰写研究计划书。
阅读路径：先判断研究计划书要解决什么问题，再学会把研究问题缩小到可操作范围，接着用人称代词回避和名词化提升学术语气，最后把宽泛主题推进为清楚的工作标题。

I. Warm-up 热身

核心问题：为什么要写研究计划书？如何选题？学术写作为何少用第一/第二人称？——因为要保持客观、非个人化的语气。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读

判断研究问题（research question）好坏的三种典型毛病：太小/太琐碎、太大/不可操作、弱的是非问题。要会诊断并改进。

A. Components of a Research Proposal 研究计划书构成

Sample Reading 先要求概括 research proposal 的组成部分。网页学习时可按下面 8 项检查自己的计划书是否完整：
ElementWhat to include
Working title把宽泛方向压缩成可操作题目，明确对象、范围、方法或语料。
Background / rationale说明研究领域为什么重要，为什么值得研究。
Literature review概述已有研究，并指出 gap、争议或尚未解决的问题。
Research question / objective提出清晰、具体、可回答的问题或目标。
Methodology说明数据来源、样本、工具、步骤、分析方法。
Feasibility / schedule说明研究能否在时间、资源和数据条件下完成。
Expected contribution说明研究可能带来的理论、实践或教学意义。
References列出计划书中已引用的主要文献。

B. Selecting a short-term research topic 短期研究选题

第二个 Sample Reading 任务要求判断成对题目中哪一个更适合 short-term research。判断标准不是“题目听起来大不大”，而是看它是否能在有限时间内完成：范围是否窄、数据是否可获得、方法是否明确、问题是否有文献意义、结论是否能落到具体对象上。

Task 2 Check whether a topic is suitable for short-term research

  1. Can the topic be narrowed to a specific population, place, corpus, period, or method?
  2. Can the data be collected or accessed during the course project?
  3. Does the topic lead to a research question rather than a broad slogan?
  4. Can the answer be supported by evidence rather than personal opinion?
显示判断原则
适合短期研究的题目通常具有 limited scope + accessible data + clear method + research value。例如 “How do different intervention programs help struggling readers in one elementary school?” 比 “How do we improve reading in elementary schools?” 更可操作。

Task 3 Decide if the following questions are appropriate for research topics. Why or why not? And how to improve them if necessary?

  1. How many cars are there visiting the Desert Vista Mall?
  2. How do we improve reading in elementary schools?
  3. Can remote sensing be used to detect impervious surfaces?
显示参考答案
QuestionJudgmentWhy?How to improve it?
How many cars are there visiting the Desert Vista Mall? Not appropriate as written.
It is too small and too trivial.		 You know how to answer this question. You know which mall to visit and what to do. The problem is that it does not address what is important in the literature. The question could become meaningful for transportation planning literature if it is related to weekend or weekday traffic, or to which mall entrance has more or less traffic.
How do we improve reading in elementary schools? Not appropriate as written.
It is too large and not doable.		 It connects to the research area of elementary school success by discussing reading in general, but it lacks any link to the research methods. Narrow it by asking how different intervention programs help struggling students, or how curricula at charter schools differ from those at public schools.
Can remote sensing be used to detect impervious surfaces? Weak yes-or-no question. It can be answered yes or no. However, some yes-or-no questions are actually acceptable when they test something specific. Here, both the literature side and the approach side lack specificity. Add details to both sides: Can Markov random fields improve classification of impervious surfaces in Quickbird imagery compared to maximum likelihood classifiers in desert cities? This stronger version suggests a history of detecting impervious surfaces with maximum-likelihood classification and identifies Markov random fields as the method to test.

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. Personal Pronouns 人称代词

  • 第一人称：用之确立作者身份；去之强化非个人化语气。学术写作通常倾向去掉。
  • 第二人称（you）：除非给指令，否则极少使用。
  • 第三人称：听起来更客观、更有说服力。

Task 1 Avoid first / second person pronouns（开放题）

  1. I think that the methodology section is a hugely important part of your research proposal.
  2. When you review literature, you should be aware that you need to identify existing gaps that your research intends to fill.
  3. I chose this method because it was less complex.
  4. My reading has shown that there are several reasons why plants are dispersed by ants.
  5. The review of recent related literature could help you discover the latest facilities ...
  6. I also found that the results of this study concurred with Berg's (1975) hypothesis ...
显示参考答案
  1. The methodology section is of great importance for the research proposal.
  2. Existing gaps should be identified that the present research intends to fill in the literature review.
  3. This method was chosen for its simplicity.
  4. According to the literature reviewed, there are several reasons why plants are dispersed by ants.
  5. In the review of the recent literature, latest facilities could be found which are more efficient than earlier existing ones in the current study.
  6. The results of this study concurred with Berg's (1975) hypothesis that seeds are protected from fire through their burial in ants' nests.
技巧：用被动语态（was chosen / should be identified）、名词化（of great importance）或换主语（According to the literature）去掉 I / you / my。

Task 2 Rewrite the stair-use paragraph by avoiding first and second person pronouns

Original focus: the present study is to investigate the impact of two simple actions aimed at getting more female employees to use the stairs at a five-floor worksite. The paragraph reports a health sign, an email from the worksite doctor, four observation stages, and the rise and later decline of stair use.

显示参考改写

The present study investigated the impact of two simple interventions designed to encourage female employees to use the stairs at a five-floor worksite. First, a health sign linking stair use to health and fitness was displayed. Second, an email was sent by the worksite doctor to emphasize the positive effects of stair use on general fitness. Stair use was observed at four stages: the baseline week, the week after the sign was posted, the week after the email was sent, and three weeks later.

The results showed that stair use increased from a baseline of 69% to 77% one week after the sign was posted and then to 89% after the email was sent. However, four weeks after the email, stair use declined to 67%. Although the interventions initially appeared to increase stair use, the effect disappeared after one month; therefore, firm recommendations cannot be made on the basis of this evidence.

B. Nominalization 名词化

名词化＝把动词/形容词变成名词（increase 动→increase 名；experience 动→the experience of…）。它使句子更抽象、更正式、更"学术腔"，是学位英语写作的高频考点。

Task 1 Noun Forms for Nominalization

Task 2 Rewrite using nominalization（开放题）

  1. Many children experience worries when they go to school for the first time.
  2. Crime was increasing rapidly and the police were becoming concerned.
  3. Elephants argue over small concerns, just like humans.
  4. We need to know which parts of our library are being used most extensively ...
  5. I know English well. I worked for three years in a factory in Shanghai. I think I am good enough for the job.
显示参考答案
  1. The experience of worries with respect to being at school for the first time is common for children.
  2. The rapid increase in crime was causing concern among the police.
  3. Arguments over small concerns are something elephants have, as well as humans.
  4. It must be found out which parts of the library are in most extensive use so that it can be determined what resources are most in need.
  5. Both my English proficiency and my three-year work experience in a factory in Shanghai qualify me to be an ideal candidate for the job.

Task 3 Rewrite a paragraph by using nominalization

本练习要求把关于澳大利亚财富分配和阶层结构的段落改写得更学术。下面是练习段落；阅读时先找出其中已经使用或可以继续强化的名词化表达，如 distribution, indicator, egalitarianism, mobility, inequity。

The inequity in the distribution of wealth in Australia is yet another indicator of Australia's lack of egalitarianism. In 1985, 20% of the Australian population owned 72.2% of the wealth, with the top 50% owning 92.1% (Raskall, 1988). Such a significant skew in the distribution of wealth indicates that, at least in economic terms, there is an established class system in Australia. McGregor (1988) argues that Australian society can be categorised into three levels: the upper, middle and working classes. In addition, most Australians continue to remain in the class into which they were born despite arguments about the ease of social mobility in Australian society. The issue of class and its inherent inequity is further compounded by factors such as race and gender within and across these class divisions.
显示改写思路
这段材料本身已经接近学术表达，重点不在重新编造事实，而在观察如何把较口语化的说法压缩成名词短语：wealth is not shared equally 可转为 inequity in the distribution of wealth；Australia is not egalitarian 可转为 Australia's lack of egalitarianism；people can move between classes 可转为 social mobility；race and gender make the class problem worse 可转为 the issue of class is compounded by race and gender。复习时要记住：名词化常把“动作/状态”变成“概念”，让句子更正式、更抽象。

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

研究计划三步走：
  • A. Selecting a topic 选题：选你真正喜欢、好奇或擅长的题目。
  • B. Formulating the focus 聚焦：把宽泛题目缩小到具体方面（特定人群、理论基础、方法）。
  • C. Establishing a working title 拟定工作标题：逐步细化。

Example Refining a working title

观察 Eric 的工作标题如何从宽泛一步步细化到可操作：

显示细化过程
  1. Explaining vulnerability
  2. Explaining vulnerability of adolescent girls
  3. Explaining vulnerability of adolescent girls in North America
  4. Explaining vulnerability of adolescent girls in North America to violence
  5. Explaining vulnerability of adolescent girls in North America to violence through empirical field work
细化方向：研究对象 → 地域 → 具体问题 → 研究方法，一步步把题目变窄、变可操作。
本单元收束：一个好的 research proposal 不是把兴趣写大，而是把问题写准、写窄、写得能研究。复习时先检查研究问题是否可操作，再看语言是否去除了过强的个人色彩，最后检查标题是否逐步聚焦。
