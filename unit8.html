UNIT 8

致谢与投稿信
Acknowledgements & Cover Letter

致谢要素 · 投稿信 · 礼貌用语 · 回复审稿意见 · 回复决定信

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解致谢的组成部分；学会撰写致谢与投稿信（submission cover letter）。
阅读路径：先区分致谢、投稿信和回复信各自的交际目的，再学习礼貌表达，随后练习逐条回复审稿意见，最后把修改说明组织成正式的 decision letter reply。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读

致谢（Acknowledgements）通常感谢：参与研究的对象/患者、提供讨论与见解的专家、匿名审稿人、提供资助的基金项目。
投稿信（Cover Letter）要素：稿件标题、研究简介与亮点、未一稿多投声明、通讯作者信息、对编辑的礼貌请求。

Task 3 Fill in the blanks in the Acknowledgements（开放题）

根据语境填入恰当的英文表达（题目给的是中文提示：感谢 / 参与 / 感激 / 匿名评审员 / 修回意见 / 资助 等）。
显示参考答案

The authors would like to thank the patients who participated in the study. We would also like to show our gratitude to Dr. Jean-Frederic Colombel for invaluable discussions and insights on Crohn's disease, and we thank three anonymous reviewers for their detailed comments. We are also immensely grateful to Dr. Emilie Grasset for her expertise and discussion on an earlier version of the manuscript. This research was supported in part by R01 DK106593 and the Sanford J. Grossman Charitable Trust; R.U. is supported by a Career Development Award from the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation and an NIH K23 Career Development Award.

关键词组：感谢 = thank / would like to thank；参与 = participated / were involved / were recruited；感激 = show our gratitude / immensely grateful to；匿名评审员 = anonymous reviewers；修回意见 = detailed comments；专长 = expertise；稿子 = manuscript；部分由…资助 = is partially supported / was supported in part by；资助 = supported / funded。

III. Language Focus 语言重点

Politeness 礼貌用语

书信/邮件写作中的礼貌，关键在于顾及对方的感受。回复审稿意见时，先致谢/致歉，再说明已如何修改。

Task 1 Make a point-by-point response to reviewers' comments（开放题）

  1. The entire manuscript needs to be edited for proper use of English and syntax.
  2. Check instructions for authors for the required journal format for referring to the literature.
  3. Avoid the use of ± to express variation.
  4. The sample size of included studies may be shown in the forest plots (Figure 2).
  5. The reference standard investigations may be briefly described.
显示参考回复
  1. We are really sorry for the inappropriate English language use. We have asked an English expert consultant to proofread the paper.
  2. We are very sorry for our incorrect reference to published literature, and have checked the instructions for authors for the required journal format and corrected the references accordingly.
  3. We have made the correction according to the reviewer's comments on the use of ± to express variation, and changed it to mean (SD).
  4. Thank you for raising this point. The sample size has been added to the forest plots in Figure 2.
  5. We have moved the section on the description of the gold standard from the appendix to the main body of the manuscript.

Task 2 Fill in the polite expressions（开放题）

显示参考答案
1) Thank you for raising this point / pointing it out.
2) Please let me know
3) Please find attached the manuscript.
4) I'd appreciate it
5) as suggested by the reviewer / according to the reviewer's comment

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Writing a Submission Cover Letter 投稿信

投稿信给编辑留下对论文的第一印象，可能影响录用决定。写作时可参考范文（Sample Reading 2）。

B. Writing a Reply to the Decision Letter 回复决定信

若决定为 major revision（大修），作者应：① 按审稿意见逐条修改；② 核对正文与参考文献的拼写与格式；③ 用"修订模式（track change）"高亮改动；④ 上传并重新提交论文。

Sample A reply to the decision letter

显示范文

Dear Editor-in-chief,

We are grateful to the editors and reviewers for their time and constructive comments on our manuscript. We have implemented their comments and suggestions and wish to submit a revised version of the manuscript for further consideration of publication in the journal. Changes in the initial version of the manuscript are either highlighted for added sentences or strikethrough for deleted sentences in the revised version. Below, we also provide a point-by-point response explaining how we have addressed each of the editors' or reviewers' comments. We look forward to the outcome of your assessment.

Yours sincerely,
On behalf of the co-authors
Ling Huchong, MD, Ph.D.

本单元收束：论文写作结束后，语言任务并没有结束；致谢要准确说明贡献，投稿信要建立编辑的第一印象，回复信要逐条、礼貌、具体地说明修改。
