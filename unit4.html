UNIT 4

研究方法
Methodology

方法部分的功能与要素 · 被动语态 · 顺序词 · 句子排序 · 问卷设计

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 学会描述研究方法；理解被动语态在学术写作中的重要性；
  • 恰当使用顺序标记词描述过程；掌握问卷设计技能。
阅读路径：先理解方法部分为什么要可重复，再识别对象、材料、程序和统计分析等信息要素，随后学习被动语态和顺序标记词，最后把零散步骤组织成可检验的方法段落。

I. Warm-up 热身：方法部分的核心概念

  • 方法部分的功能：提供判断研究效度（validity）的依据。须清晰精确描述实验如何进行及选择该流程的理由，以便：①他人可重复实验验证可重现性；②读者可判断结果与结论是否有效。
  • 八个信息要素：overview, subjects, location, materials, sampling techniques, procedure, statistical treatment, possible restrictions。
  • method vs methodology：methodology 是对方法的系统性理论分析，method 是具体方法本身。
  • 数据类型：按收集方式分 primary / secondary；按性质分 quantitative / qualitative。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读：方法部分的信息要素

Sample Reading 要求从方法部分中找出具体信息。方法部分的核心不是“写得多”，而是让读者能判断研究是否有效、是否可重复。

Task Answer questions based on the sample methodology

显示参考答案
  1. Paragraph 1 is about sample collection.
  2. The hospital controls may not be completely representative of the study population because they are not a random sample from the whole target population. Compared with a true random sample, hospital-based controls might underestimate the actual effects.
  3. When recruiting samples, a questionnaire was designed to collect information including personal background, lifestyle factors, genetic risk factors, occupational and environmental exposures, reproduction and sexual status, medical history, and physical activity.

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. The Passive Voice 被动语态

被动语态在学术写作中广泛使用，因为主观色彩更少。三种推荐使用被动的情形：
  • ① 不知道或不关心动作执行者是谁；
  • ② 关注动作的接受者而非执行者；
  • ③ 希望保持中立、客观的立场。
方法部分高频被动动词：was performed, was designed, were recruited, were collected, were frozen, were analyzed, was measured, were extracted, was used, were calculated。

B. Sequential Markers 顺序标记词

过程段按时间/逻辑顺序展开，常用顺序词：
开头 To begin with… / First of all…；中间 Secondly… / Next… / After that… / In addition…；结尾 Finally… / Lastly…

Task Rewrite the paper-making process into a cohesive paragraph（开放题）

显示参考段落
Firstly, the logs are placed in the shredder. Then they are cut into small chips which are mixed with water and acid. After that, they are heated and crushed to a heavy pulp which is cleaned and chemically bleached to whiten it. Later, it is passed through rollers to flatten it, and sheets of wet paper are produced. IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Identifying Information Elements 辨识信息要素

方法部分通常包含的信息要素（定义）：overview（概述）、subjects（研究对象）、location（研究地点）、restrictions（为保证数据有效的限制/预防措施）、sampling（抽样方法）、materials（材料）、procedure（流程步骤）、statistical treatment（统计处理）。
八要素中，唯一始终必须包含的只有 materials 和 procedure。常考判断/填空点。

Task The following sentences are taken from method sections of different research articles. Identify what information element is presented.（开放题）

显示参考答案
1) subjects　2) material　3) procedure　4) overview　5) statistical treatment　6) procedure and material　7) sampling　8) research location

B. Reordering Sentences 句子排序

Research methodology involves: identifying the method, specifying subjects, selecting a representative sample, selecting reliable instruments, describing the procedure, collecting data, and analyzing/interpreting results.

C. Turning Notes into a Passage 把笔记改写成方法段落

方法部分不只罗列步骤，还要解释选择理由。写作时注意：定性/定量方法的选择要说明理由；样本量和抽样方式要说明理由；访谈题或问卷要说明理由；数据分析方法和软件也要说明理由。

Task Write a method section for a beverage taste test

显示参考段落

A taste test was conducted to determine students' preferences and attitudes toward sugar-sweetened and artificially-sweetened beverages. Ten people, five male students and five female students, from Science English Class 1 were chosen at random to participate in the test. The materials used were thirty straws, two cups, one blindfold, and two containers of Kool-Aid. One container held four cups of orange Kool-Aid sweetened with one half cup of sugar. The other container held four cups of orange Kool-Aid sweetened with nine packets of Nutrasweet artificial sweetener.

The steps included in the test were as follows. The students were placed into two lines, one for males and one for females. Alternately, men and women completed the taste test. A blindfold was first fastened over the eyes of the tester. Then the two cups were filled with beverage, one with artificially-sweetened Kool-Aid and the other with sugar-sweetened Kool-Aid. The tester answered three survey questions, took a sip from each cup with a straw, and then answered the final two questions. Finally, the remaining drink was thrown away, and the next tester repeated the process.

D. Gathering Information for a Method Section 收集方法部分信息

写方法部分前应先收集：研究材料、材料准备方式、research protocol、测量与计算方式、用于分析数据的统计检验。本单元的小组任务是围绕 “students' attendance in English class” 设计方法部分。

E. Designing a Questionnaire 问卷设计

设计问卷的八个步骤（重点记忆）：
  • ① Determine the information required
  • ② Define the target respondents
  • ③ Choose the method(s) of reaching respondents
  • ④ Design questions
  • ⑤ Put questions into a meaningful order and format
  • ⑥ Check the length of the questionnaire
  • ⑦ Pre-test the questionnaire
  • ⑧ Develop the final survey form
好问卷的标准：能有效准确收集数据、便于数据处理与制表、不收集非必要信息、便于全面有意义的分析。问题分 open（开放式）与 closed（封闭式）两类。

Sample Class Attendance Survey

问卷样例以 Class Attendance Survey 为题，开头先说明研究目的、匿名和保密原则，再让学生用打勾或填写空格作答。
显示关键题目设计
  • 询问上大学原因：natural progression; qualification for long-term job prospects; personal fulfillment; other reasons.
  • 询问通勤时间：less than a quarter hour; a quarter to half an hour; half an hour to an hour; more than two hours.
  • 询问是否有工作：Yes / No.
  • 询问最常见缺课原因：students choose the main three reasons and rank them 1 to 3.
  • 询问补救行为：contact the lecturer, get handouts, ask a classmate, borrow notes, look on the student portal, or take no action.
  • 开放题：suggest changes the university could make to influence attendance at lectures.
本单元收束：方法部分的核心是让读者相信研究过程清楚、可重复、可验证。下一单元会在这个基础上处理研究完成后的写法：如何呈现结果并解释其意义。
