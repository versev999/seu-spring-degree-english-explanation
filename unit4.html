UNIT 4

研究方法
Methodology

方法部分的功能与要素 · 被动语态 · 顺序词 · 句子排序 · 问卷设计

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 学会描述研究方法；理解被动语态在学术写作中的重要性；
  • 恰当使用顺序标记词描述过程；掌握问卷设计技能。

I. Warm-up 热身：方法部分的核心概念

  • 方法部分的功能：提供判断研究效度（validity）的依据。须清晰精确描述实验如何进行及选择该流程的理由，以便：①他人可重复实验验证可重现性；②读者可判断结果与结论是否有效。
  • 八个信息要素：overview, subjects, location, materials, sampling techniques, procedure, statistical treatment, possible restrictions。
  • method vs methodology：methodology 是对方法的系统性理论分析，method 是具体方法本身。
  • 数据类型：按收集方式分 primary / secondary；按性质分 quantitative / qualitative。

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. The Passive Voice 被动语态

被动语态在学术写作中广泛使用，因为主观色彩更少。三种推荐使用被动的情形：
  • ① 不知道或不关心动作执行者是谁；
  • ② 关注动作的接受者而非执行者；
  • ③ 希望保持中立、客观的立场。
方法部分高频被动动词：was performed, was designed, were recruited, were collected, were frozen, were analyzed, was measured, were extracted, was used, were calculated。

Task 2 Rewrite the text using the passive voice（开放题）

(the "poison ivy" passage)

显示参考改写
A poison ivy infection is considered by some to be humorous. But it is not funny at all. A rash ... is caused by contact with the plant. Poison ivy infection has been studied by scientists for centuries, but no preventive pill ... has been found. The poisonous substance ... is called urushiol. After the skin is touched by urushiol, the exposed area will soon be covered by blisters and weeping sores.

B. Sequential Markers 顺序标记词

过程段按时间/逻辑顺序展开，常用顺序词：
开头 To begin with… / First of all…；中间 Secondly… / Next… / After that… / In addition…；结尾 Finally… / Lastly…

Task Rewrite the paper-making process into a cohesive paragraph（开放题）

显示参考段落
Firstly, the logs are placed in the shredder. Then they are cut into small chips which are mixed with water and acid. After that, they are heated and crushed to a heavy pulp which is cleaned and chemically bleached to whiten it. Later, it is passed through rollers to flatten it, and sheets of wet paper are produced. Finally, the water is removed from the sheets, which are pressed, dried and refined until the finished paper is produced.

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Identifying Information Elements 辨识信息要素

方法部分通常包含的信息要素（定义）：overview（概述）、subjects（研究对象）、location（研究地点）、restrictions（为保证数据有效的限制/预防措施）、sampling（抽样方法）、materials（材料）、procedure（流程步骤）、statistical treatment（统计处理）。
八要素中，唯一始终必须包含的只有 materials 和 procedure。常考判断/填空点。

Task The following sentences are taken from method sections of different research articles. Identify what information element is presented.（开放题）

显示参考答案
1) subjects　2) material　3) procedure　4) overview　5) statistical treatment　6) procedure and material　7) sampling　8) research location

B. Reordering Sentences 句子排序

Research methodology involves: identifying the method, specifying subjects, selecting a representative sample, selecting reliable instruments, describing the procedure, collecting data, and analyzing/interpreting results.

显示正确顺序

E. Designing a Questionnaire 问卷设计

设计问卷的八个步骤（重点记忆）：
  • ① Determine the information required
  • ② Define the target respondents
  • ③ Choose the method(s) of reaching respondents
  • ④ Design questions
  • ⑤ Put questions into a meaningful order and format
  • ⑥ Check the length of the questionnaire
  • ⑦ Pre-test the questionnaire
  • ⑧ Develop the final survey form
好问卷的标准：能有效准确收集数据、便于数据处理与制表、不收集非必要信息、便于全面有意义的分析。问题分 open（开放式）与 closed（封闭式）两类。
