UNIT 3

引言与文献综述
Introduction & Literature Review

引言要素 · 引用时态 · 引用动词 · 引用方式 · 避免抄袭

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解引言部分的一般功能；学会引用他人前期工作；
  • 熟悉文献综述的写法；学会通过释义（paraphrasing）避免抄袭。
阅读路径：先看引言如何提出研究位置，再看文献综述如何连接前人研究与自己的问题，随后学习引用时态、引用动词和避免抄袭，最后把引用从“罗列文献”推进到“服务自己的研究”。

I. Warm-up 课前思考

A. Introduction Think about the role of an introduction before reading the samples.

  1. What are the general functions of the introduction section?
  2. What do you think is the most important function of the introduction section?
  3. What are probably included in the introduction section?
先带着这三个问题读后面的引言范例：引言不是简单介绍背景，而是逐步说明研究领域、研究空白、研究目标和论文意义。

B. Literature Review Connect previous studies with your own research focus.

  1. What is the purpose of writing a literature review?
  2. What steps may be taken in conducting a literature review?
  3. How do we cite other people’s previous work in our own research articles?
文献综述的重点不是把文献排成清单，而是说明前人研究已经做了什么、还缺什么，以及你的研究如何接上这个缺口。

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. Tense in Citation 引用时态

  • 一般现在时：最常用，引用他人观点、陈述文献/理论/方法，强调研究的现实意义。
  • 一般过去时：常用于整合式引用，讲述具体事件、所得结果。
  • 现在完成时：表明结果较新，强调前人研究与本研究的直接相关性。

Task Check Sample 2 and complete the tense table.

QuestionAnswerPurpose

Are some sentences written in the present perfect tense?
Which tense is used more?
Sample 2 中三种时态都出现，但一般现在时最多，因为文献综述常用它来呈现他人观点、理论和研究的当前意义。过去时用于具体事件或结果；现在完成时强调已有研究到现在仍直接相关。

Task Which tense is used most in citation, and why?

Which tense is most commonly used for citation in academic writing?

用得最多的是一般现在时，因为它强调研究的当下意义（present significance）。过去时用于具体结果/事件；现在完成时强调结果之新与相关性。

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Citing Previous Research 引用前人研究

三种引用方式（重点区分）：
  • Information prominent 信息突出式：重点在信息，作者姓名放句末括号。
  • Author prominent 作者突出式：把作者作为句子主语。
  • Weak author prominent 弱作者突出式：用 "Several authors have reported that…" 概括多位作者，姓名放句末括号。
课本示例（供对照理解三种引用方式）：
· Information prominent："...the wool industry is experiencing difficulties related to falling demand worldwide since the development of high-quality synthetic fibers (Smith, 2000)."
· Author prominent："As Smith (2000) pointed out, the wool industry was experiencing difficulties related to falling demand..."
· Weak author prominent："Several authors have reported that the wool industry is experiencing difficulties... (Nguyen, 2005; Smith, 2000; Wilson, 2003)."

Task Read the following statements, decide which kind of citation is used, and then rewrite each of them in another kind of citation.（开放题）

显示参考思路
判断要点：作者姓名出现在句末括号＝信息突出式；作者作为句子主语/出发点（As X pointed out / X found that）＝作者突出式；用 "Several authors have reported that…" 概括多位作者＝弱作者突出式。改写时在这三种之间转换即可（如把信息突出式改为 "As Smith (2000) pointed out, ..." 的作者突出式）。

Practice Citation type and rewriting

  1. Several authors have suggested that automated testing should be more readily accepted (Balcer, 1989; Stahl, 1989; Carver and Tai, 1991).
  2. For viscoelastic fluids, the behavior of the time-dependent stresses in the transient shear flows is also very important (Boger et al., 1994).
  3. Close (1983) developed a simplified theory using an analogy between heat and mass transfer and the equivalent heat transfer only case.
显示参考答案
  1. Weak author prominent citation. It can be rewritten as: It has been suggested that automated testing should be more readily accepted (Balcer, 1989; Stahl, 1989; Carver and Tai, 1991).
  2. Information prominent citation. It can be rewritten as: Boger et al. (1994) claim that, for viscoelastic fluids, the behaviour of the time-dependent stresses in the transient shear flows is also very important.
  3. Author prominent citation. It can be rewritten as: A simplified theory has been developed using an analogy between heat and mass transfer and the equivalent heat transfer only case (Close, 1983).

B. Relating Literature to Your Research 把文献与自己的研究联系起来

判断一篇文献综述是否真正服务自己的研究，可以依次问这 10 个问题：
  1. What do we already know in the immediate area concerned?
  2. What are the characteristics of the key concepts or the main factors or variables?
  3. What are the relationships between these key concepts, factors or variables?
  4. What are the existing theories?
  5. Where are the inconsistencies or other shortcomings in our knowledge and understanding?
  6. What views need to be further tested?
  7. What evidence is lacking, inconclusive, contradictory or too limited?
  8. Why study further the research problem?
  9. What contribution can the present study be expected to make?
  10. What research designs or methods seem unsatisfactory?
网页阅读时可以把这 10 个问题当作检查表：如果综述只回答“谁做过什么”，却没有回答“还缺什么、为什么还要研究”，它就还不是合格的 literature review。

Task Is the sample a good literature review? Why?（开放题）

PPT questionReference answer

显示参考答案
不是一篇好综述。它只总结前人研究，没有评价，也没展示不同理论/观点之间的关系。读完后仍不知哪些理论重要、哪些没定论、有何不足。它按"研究者"而非"研究内容"组织、并按时间排列，强调了人而非其工作，导致难以比较、评价。主要问题：没有展示前人研究与作者自己研究问题的关系。

C. Avoiding Plagiarism 避免抄袭

引入他人观点而不抄袭的三种方法：Quoting（引用原话）、Paraphrasing（释义）、Summarizing（概括）。
区别：summary 比原文短得多；paraphrase 与原文长度相近甚至更长。

Task 1 Acceptable or unacceptable paraphrase?

Original (Siegel, 1986): We do not yet understand all the ways in which brain chemicals are related to emotions and thoughts, but the salient point is that our state of mind has an immediate and direct effect on our state of body.

Paraphrase 1: "Siegel (1986) writes that although the relationship between brain chemistry and thoughts and feelings is not fully understood, we do know that our psychological state affects our physical state."

可接受 ✓：使用了同义词、改变了句子结构和语态，并标注了出处。

Paraphrase 2: "Siegel (1986) writes that we still do not know all the ways in which brain chemistry is related to emotions and thoughts, but the important point is that our mental state has an immediate and direct effect on our physical state."

不可接受 ✗：句子结构与原文完全相同，只换了个别同义词，其余措辞照搬。即使提了出处，仍属抄袭。

Paraphrase 3: "According to Siegel (1986), our mind affects our body quickly and directly, although we do not yet understand every aspect of how brain chemicals relate to emotions and thoughts."

不可接受 ✗：虽改变了结构，但 key phrases 直接照搬原文。即使提了出处，仍属抄袭。
合格释义三要素：①换同义词 ②改句子结构/语态 ③标注出处。三者缺一不可。

Task 2 Paraphrase the sentences（开放题）

  1. The bomb exploded and caused many casualties.
  2. Among all the feelings of mankind, love is probably the noblest.
  3. With no doubt, these firms are doing their part in educating the next generation of thinkers.
  4. No one knows how many CEOs suffer from alcoholism and drug abuse, but estimates run as high as 30%.
  5. There is general consensus among European leaders that total unification of European countries would strengthen the economic stability of the entire region.
  6. Attitudes toward the elderly are changing dramatically as baby boomers grow older. For many years the elderly were seen as less useful once they reached retirement age. However, the new research points to the increased productivity of aged workers, even those who are in their 80s and 90s.
显示参考答案
  1. The bomb explosion led to a great number of injuries and deaths.
  2. There is probably no other feeling that is nobler than love.
  3. It cannot be denied that these firms are participating in turning out the future thinkers.
  4. The estimated percentage of CEOs who are alcoholics and abuse drugs is up to 30%.
  5. European leaders generally agree that if European countries would unify, there would be a more stable economy.
  6. New research indicates that older aged workers are productive, even into their 80s and 90s. As a result, attitudes toward the elderly are "changing dramatically" from the view that the elderly were no longer "useful" once they retired.

D. Writing a Literature Review 六步法

  • Step 1: Decide on your areas of research（确定研究领域）
  • Step 2: Search for the literature（检索文献）
  • Step 3: Find relevant excerpts（找相关片段：结论发现 / 术语定义 / 后续研究呼吁 / 文献空白 / 分歧）
  • Step 4: Encode the literature（编码文献）
  • Step 5: Create your conceptual schema（建立概念框架）
  • Step 6: Write your literature review（撰写综述）
本单元收束：引言和文献综述的重点不是堆文献，而是说明研究领域、研究空白和本研究的必要性。复习时按“引言功能 → 引用时态 → 引用动词 → 释义与概括 → 六步法”的顺序梳理。
