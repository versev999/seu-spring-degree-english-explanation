UNIT 5

结果与讨论
Results & Discussion

要素辨识 · 比较与对比 · 因果关系 · 图表描述 · 强化/弱化论断

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解结果与讨论部分的功能与要素；学会描述图表信息；
  • 掌握比较与对比、因果关系的技巧；学会强化与弱化论断。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读

定位结果（locate results）的常用动词：discovered, shows, found, displayed, showed, demonstrated。

Task 3 Match the information element with each sentence (Discussion)

选项：A. possible explanations；B. the new finding different from previous studies；C. reference to the main purpose / research questions；D. review of the most important finding(s)；E. comparison with expected results & other studies；F. a summary of the finding(s)。选好后点"检查答案"。
1. The first research question investigated how learners' choice of language for thinking influences lexical collocation production.
2. Statistically significant results revealed that learners primarily using Chinese mingled with English had the lowest inaccuracy rate...
3. This finding accords with Goh's (2002) assumption that translation as a cognitive tactic slows down cognitive processing...
4. Another crucial implication ... is that metacognitive skill training should be incorporated into ESL/EFL classrooms.
5. This study indicated an interesting finding: thinking mainly in English was not beneficial for oral production of lexical collocations...
6. To sum up, comparing the inaccuracy rates in the three tasks indicates that processing demands may influence the quality of talk.
参考答案：1-D　2-C　3-B　4-A　5-E　6-F。讨论部分通常按"回顾发现→点明研究问题→新发现→可能解释→与他人比较→总结"的逻辑展开。

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. Comparison and Contrast 比较与对比

  • Comparison（比较）—表相似；Contrast（对比）—表差异。
  • 表比较：like, similar, both, as well as, in the same way, have…in common, the same as, similarly, likewise, also。
  • 表对比：although, whereas, but, instead, unlike, be different from, conversely, on the contrary, on the other hand, while, however。
三条提示：①不必所有信息都两两比较；②不必对每个变化都同等强调；③比较/对比须有具体相关数据支撑。

B. Cause and Effect 因果关系

因果常用表达：because (of), due to（因为）；result from（由…造成）；result in / lead to / cause（导致）；therefore, causing。

Task Fill in the missing causal words

从词库为每个空选择恰当的因果词，点"检查答案"。词库：because of / can lead to / causing / causes / due to。
(1) sleep is often interrupted ___ the pain.
(2) Such drugs ___ other negative effects.
(3) Drugs can inhibit productivity, perhaps even ___ regular absences.
(4) the unpredictable occurrence of these headaches ___ disruption in family life.
(5) many of these people feel discouraged and even depressed ___ the cycle of misery.
(1) because of　(2) can lead to　(3) causing　(4) causes　(5) due to。注意 because of / due to 后接名词；cause / lead to 作谓语；causing 作非谓语补充说明结果。

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Describing the Graphic Information 描述图表

图表描述三步法：Step 1 简介图表并指出主趋势（地点、时间、内容、目的）；Step 2 描述最重要的数据，必要时比较；Step 3 总结趋势。
常用词汇：a slight/gradual/steady rise；a rapid increase；a marked/dramatic/steep/sharp decrease；a sudden fluctuation；the peak（峰值）；bottomed out（触底）；an upward / downward trend（上升/下降趋势）。突出数据：It is apparent/clear/obvious from Table 2 that…

Task Describe the line graph of Ford car production（开放题）

显示参考表述
  • the bottom of the line → in January（约 1.2%，3 月触底 bottomed out）
  • the peak → in December（达到 6.3%）
  • the fluctuating part → from May to September（在 2.8%–4.6% 间波动）
整体：The graph shows an upward trend in the growth rate of car production for Ford from January to December 2018, revealing two important rising periods during the year.

B. Strengthening or Weakening Statements 强化与弱化论断（Hedging）

  • 证据充分明确→强化（strengthen）；证据不太确定→弱化（weaken/hedge）。
  • 强化用词：show, prove, establish, confirm, conclude；clearly, obviously, undoubtedly, certainly；crucial, fundamental, significant, strong。
  • 弱化用词：appear, seem, suggest, speculate, estimate；likely, maybe, perhaps, possibly, probably, seemingly, somewhat。

Task 1 Is each sentence strengthening or weakening a claim?

第 1 句为示例（strengthening）。判断其余各句，点选项即时反馈。

2) We observed a stronger positive association for...

stronger positive association 语气肯定 → 强化。

3) It is possible that the referent group had a smaller-than-expected cancer incidence by chance.

It is possible that... 表不确定 → 弱化。

4) We observed a suggestion of an elevated risk for advanced prostate cancer with both meat types.

a suggestion of... 措辞谨慎 → 弱化。

6) Associations between saturated fat and cancer are likely to be related to energy balance...

likely 表可能性 → 弱化。

7) This could explain some of the inconsistencies in the literature...

could 表推测 → 弱化。

8) It lends strong support to...

strong support 语气肯定 → 强化。

9) We found a positive association between red meat intake and cancers of the esophagus and liver...

We found a positive association 直接断言 → 强化。

Task 2 Why did the authors weaken the claims?（开放题）

显示参考答案
作者弱化论断，是因为他们在解释结果的可能原因，对成因并不确定。此时为保持客观（keep objective），倾向于弱化论断。

C. Outlining the Results and Discussion 谋篇：四个 Move

  • Move 1: Prepare the information（检查是否回答所有研究问题、逻辑组织结果、重读综述与引言）
  • Move 2: Report results（用统计与实例呈现，定位并清晰描述发现）
  • Move 3: Comment on the results（确立意义与价值，含解释、强项、局限、可推广性）
  • Move 4: Summarize（论文中可选，学位论文中必需）
