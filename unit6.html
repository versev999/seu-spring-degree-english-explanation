UNIT 6

结论
Conclusion

结论的功能 · 概括 · 释义 · 常用句型库 · 重述研究目标

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解概括在学术写作中的重要性；熟练运用释义；
  • 能重述论点（thesis statement）；能写出逻辑严密的结论。
阅读路径：先明确结论不是简单重复，而是回看研究、概括发现、指出意义和限制；再练习 summarizing 与 paraphrasing，最后用结论句型重述研究目标并提出后续研究方向。

I & II. 结论的功能与要素

结论（conclusion）的四大功能：
  • ① Summarize / "looking back"：总结、汇总全文要点；
  • ② Analyze and evaluate：为读者分析、评价要点，说明研究为何重要；
  • ③ Implications & limitations：指出总体启示与可能局限；
  • ④ Recommendations & future directions：提出改进建议、展望未来方向。
两条铁律：① 结论是对要点的有力重述；② 绝不能因为临时想到就在结尾加入全新观点！

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. Summarizing 概括

概括＝用自己的话写出原文的缩短版，陈述要点、略去非必要内容。概括不只是复述，还要分析信息、区分主次。
技巧：①先读并标出要点 ②再读做笔记、略去例子证据 ③换词改述 ④不加入自己的观点 ⑤标注原始出处。

Task 1 Read a student summary and discuss its problems

A student wrote: The writer feels women in Canada are luckier than Indian women, because most Canadians throw out the conception of sexism. However, some Canadian females miss the good old days. In the past, gentlemen opened doors for ladies and pulled out their chairs. Now Canadian men believe women are equal and they must pay at restaurant.

  1. Where is the writer from?
  2. Have all Canadians abandoned sexist ideas, or only most Canadians?
  3. What is the difference between “pull out chairs for women” and “pull out women's chairs”?
  4. Do women today always have to pay for their meals when dining out?
显示参考概括
According to an Indian who has lived in Canada for several years, some Canadian women feel nostalgic about the days when they received special courtesies from men. Now, most Canadians endeavor to treat men and women equally.

Task Which version is an acceptable summary?

Four student versions summarize the same passage. Which one is an acceptable summary?

Version 4 可接受。Version 1 = 抄袭（仅改动词形式、调换语序，无引用）；Version 2 = 抄袭（用"引用+页码"误导，其余仍照搬）；Version 3 = 释义+引用，从抄袭角度可接受，但所引句子平庸不重要——只在专家原话或无法更好表达时才引用。

B. Paraphrasing 释义

释义＝用自己的话完整再现原意（绝不加入自己观点）。两种方式：①词层转换（保留原句法）；②深层结构转换（更大改动）。
指南：用同义词；改变词性；做必要结构调整；在肯定/否定间转换；标注原始出处。

Task Paraphrase the paragraph about family and schooling

Aries (1968) claims that starting in the 15th century people's viewpoints on family and the actual reality of family changed. However, the change was so slow and subtle that people at that time did not see it. But the growing importance of school was quite striking. In the Middle Ages children were educated from the age of seven by being placed out or apprenticed to other families. Once schools were no longer limited to religious study, they replaced apprenticeship as society's means of educating the young and initiating them into society.

显示改写提示
改写时可把 people's viewpoints on family and the actual reality of family changed 改为 ideas about family life and the organization of family life gradually shifted；把 the growing importance of school was quite striking 改为 the increasing role of schooling was much more visible。注意保留 Aries (1968) 的出处，不添加自己的评价。

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. 结论各部分常用句型库（高分句型，建议背诵）

① 总结发现：This study has shown that…；These findings suggest that in general…；One of the significant findings to emerge from this study is that…；The results of this investigation show that…
② 研究意义：These findings enhance our understanding of…；The current findings add to a growing body of literature on…；The present study makes several noteworthy contributions to…
③ 研究局限：Finally, a number of limitations need to be considered. First,…；The most important limitation lies in the fact that…；However, with a small sample size, caution must be applied…
④ 后续研究建议：Further work needs to be done to establish whether…；It would be interesting to assess the effects of…；Future research should therefore concentrate on the investigation of…
⑤ 启示/实践建议：The current study has a number of important implications for future practice.；An implication of these findings is that both X and Y should be taken into account when…

B. Restating the Research Objective 重述研究目标

结论中要重述论点，但要避免原样重复（否则枯燥）。善用同义词替换。
常用句型：This study set out to determine…；The present study was designed to determine the effect of…；In this investigation, the aim was to assess…；The purpose of the current study was to determine…；Returning to the hypothesis posed at the beginning of this study, it is now possible to state that…

Task Write concluding statements based on research questions

  1. The present study is designed to determine what in San Francisco attracts visitors more: the magnificent location, the theaters and art galleries, or its fine restaurants.
  2. The purpose of this investigation is to explore whether employees as well as managers need to be equally trained for working in the Australian multicultural workplace.
  3. This study set out to tackle the rate of juvenile delinquency in 2014 in the US.
  4. The aim of this study is to determine whether education plays a role in reducing juvenile delinquents' return to crime.
  5. The project undertaken is to evaluate the marketing strategies currently applied by the Coca-Cola Company.
  6. The current study aims to determine whether children sent to daycare or preschool start kindergarten showing more highly developed language skills.
  7. This project is aimed to explore how the discovery of a genetic basis for obesity may change the way obese persons are treated medically and socially.
显示写作方法
把题干中的 is designed to determine / aims to explore / set out to tackle 换成结论句型，如 The findings of this study indicate that..., This investigation has shown that..., Returning to the question posed at the beginning, it is now possible to state that...，并避免逐字重复研究目标。

C. 写结论的注意事项

  • 不要从含观测误差的数据中得出结论；
  • 不在不理解推导与假设的情况下使用数学公式；
  • 避免把事实与观点/影响混淆；
  • 不要从过少数据、或基于外推曲线得出过宽的结论；
  • 务必对结论加以限定（qualify），说明其适用范围与可能偏差。

Task Write a conclusion from a discussion section

The discussion argues that interventions designed to affect children's environmental identities should be situated in outdoor natural settings, include free-choice learning and detailed observation, address children's comfort levels, and create time for reflection on their relationship with the natural world.

显示参考写作方向
A suitable conclusion should summarize the main finding, state the practical implication for teachers and environmental educators, and avoid overgeneralization. It may conclude that outdoor learning experiences can strengthen children's environmental identities, foster their interest in school science, and support pro-environmental behavior when implemented in school contexts with appropriate reflection and support.
本单元收束：结论要把研究的发现、意义、局限和未来方向收拢起来，不能临时加入新观点。复习时先区分 summary 和 paraphrase，再用句型库检查结论是否包含发现、意义、局限和后续方向。
