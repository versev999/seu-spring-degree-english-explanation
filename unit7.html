UNIT 7

摘要与参考文献
Abstract & References

摘要要素 · 动词与时态 · 关键词 · 写摘要流程 · 参考文献样式

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解摘要的要素与特征；了解摘要中动词与时态的使用；
  • 学会提供关键词；理解参考文献样式；能写摘要并制作参考文献列表。
阅读路径：先拆摘要的信息要素，再看动词、时态和关键词如何帮助读者快速判断论文内容，随后按流程组装摘要，最后检查参考文献是否能让读者准确找到来源。

I. Warm-up 课前思考

Questions Use these questions to locate what an abstract must do.

  1. Where can you find an abstract in a thesis or dissertation?
  2. What is the purpose of writing an abstract?
  3. What are the basic elements for an academic abstract?
  4. What language problems may you have in abstract writing? (For example, the wording problem, the tense problem, the voice problem, etc.)
先明确摘要的位置、目的、信息要素和语言问题，后面再看样例摘要如何用有限篇幅交代背景、目的、方法、结果和意义。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读

摘要的信息要素（abstract elements）：a. background　b. objective　c. materials　d. approach　e. findings　f. conclusion　g. implication　h. limitation

Task 1 What is the most suitable subject for the sample abstract?

Choose the most suitable subject area.

样例摘要研究 academic textbooks 的 preface section，材料来自 linguistics and applied linguistics，因此最适合归入 Applied Linguistics。

Task 2 Answer questions based on the sample abstract

  1. How was the study carried out?
  2. What did the study find?
  3. What is the use of the findings?
显示参考答案
  1. Using a move structure analysis, four moves were identified to account for the different rhetorical purposes employed by writers of preface sections.
  2. It found the textual strategies used by writers to achieve the rhetorical purposes of the preface sections.
  3. The findings could be incorporated into instructional literature for writers starting book projects and may help EFL teachers select materials through external evaluation of academic textbooks.

Task 3 Match the element(s) with each sentence（开放题，含组合答案）

  1. This paper presents a genre analysis of the preface section in academic textbooks, specifically twenty-two preface sections from the closely-related fields of linguistics and applied linguistics.
  2. With the adoption of the move structure analysis proposed by Swales (1990) and Bhatia (1993), four moves were identified to account for the different rhetorical purposes employed by writers of preface sections to create a niche and establish the importance of their textbooks. These moves comprise: establishing the needs of the readership, establishing their orientations like purpose and audiences, outlining of the scope of the chapters, and finally acknowledgments.
  3. This move structure reflects the textual strategies used by writers to achieve the rhetorical purposes of the preface sections.
  4. It is suggested that these findings could be incorporated into instructional literature for writers wishing to start book projects, and that they may also be helpful for English as a Foreign Language (EFL) teachers in selecting materials for their classrooms based on their external evaluation of academic textbooks.
显示参考答案
1) a + b（background + objective）　2) c + d（materials + approach）　3) e（findings）　4) f + g（conclusion + implication）

III. Language Focus 语言重点

摘要中的动词与时态

摘要常用动词：analyze, investigate, evaluate, show, suggest, enhance。句型：…are analyzed…；…were investigated.；…was evaluated by…；This work enhances…

Task 1 Read the sample abstract and find out the verbs and sentence patterns.

VerbSentence pattern
analyze... are analyzed ...
investigate... were investigated.
evaluate... was evaluated by ...
showAlthough ... showed ..., ... showed ...
suggest... suggests ...
enhanceThis work enhances ...
PPT 这里不是只讲“摘要用什么时态”，还要求学生从 sample abstract 中找出动词和句型。复习时要同时记动词和它常搭配的句式。

Task 2 Which tense is used for each abstract element?

为摘要各要素选择时态（present / past），点"检查答案"。
Background 背景
Methods 方法
Results 结果
Conclusion 结论
Significance 意义
记忆口诀：背景 / 结论 / 意义用现在时，方法 / 结果用过去时。
ElementExample from PPT
BackgroundThe electrical and piezo-resistive responses of recycled carbon fiber (RCF)-reinforced concrete are analyzed in this article.
MethodsTwo different PAN-based RCFs were investigated. Piezo-resistivity was evaluated by monitoring the variation in the applied DC voltage.
ResultsAlthough both plain and RCF-reinforced concrete samples showed piezo-resistive responses, the latter showed increased signal-to-noise-ratio.
ConclusionThe electrical behavior suggests a mixed control owing to both ionic and electronic conductivity.
SignificanceThis work enhances the possibility of generalizing the use of smart cementitious materials in the civil engineering industry.

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

掌握摘要要素、动词和时态后，下一步不是直接写成品，而是先按流程收集信息、筛掉多余内容，再把内容压缩成一个可投稿的摘要段落。

A. Preparing for Writing an Abstract 写摘要的流程

Task Rearrange the following statements to form a reasonable process of writing an abstract.

为每个步骤选择它在流程中的次序（1–8）。第 3 句已知为第 1 步、第 7 句已知为第 8 步。选好后点"检查答案"。
Step statementOrder
1) Identify the major results from the discussion or results section.
2) Check to see if it meets the guidelines of the targeted journal.
3) Identify the major objectives and conclusions. (= Step 1)
4) Remove extra words and phrases.
5) Identify phrases with keywords in the method section.
6) Assemble the information into a single paragraph with appropriate verbs, tenses, and patterns.
7) Give the abstract to a colleague and ask whether it makes sense. (= Step 8)
8) Revise the paragraph so that the abstract conveys only the essential information.
正确顺序（按步骤）：① 句3 目标与结论 → ② 句5 方法关键词 → ③ 句1 主要结果 → ④ 句6 组装成段 → ⑤ 句4 删除多余词 → ⑥ 句8 修改至只含必要信息 → ⑦ 句2 核对期刊要求 → ⑧ 句7 请同事审读。

B. Outlining an Abstract 摘要提纲

Task Match the paper detail to its abstract element

这道题对应 PPT 中“根据论文内容填写摘要提纲”的任务。先判断每句话承担什么信息功能，再展开参考提纲。
Paper detailAbstract element
With the development of Information Technology, the Internet is accessible to all, especially to students.
To study the network-based English phonetics teaching mode for English majors.
By selecting college English majors of 2017 in Anhui Institute of Information Technology as subjects, the author collects and analyzes the data.
Students who learned on the net had better English pronunciation than those learning in the traditional mode.
Teachers are advised to use the network-based teaching mode to improve teaching and learning effectiveness.
摘要提纲不是按原文顺序抄句子，而是先识别信息功能：背景说明研究场景，目标说明要研究什么，方法说明对象和处理方式，结果说明发现，结论/启示说明可采取的做法。
显示参考提纲
Information elementExample from the lesson
BackgroundWith the development of Information Technology, the Internet is accessible to all, especially to students. English teaching is changing from textbooks, chalk and blackboard to multimedia, apps and other tools.
Research objectiveTo study the network-based English phonetics teaching mode for English majors.
MethodsBy selecting college English majors of 2017 in Anhui Institute of Information Technology as subjects, the author collects and analyzes the data.
ResultsStudents who learned on the net had better English pronunciation than those learning in the traditional mode.
Conclusion / implicationTeachers are advised to use the network-based teaching mode to improve teaching and learning effectiveness.

C. Drafting an Abstract Together with Keywords 摘要成稿与关键词

Sample Draft an abstract together with keywords

显示摘要范文

Abstract—With the development of Information Technology, the Internet is accessible to all, especially to the students. Based on the network, English teaching is changing greatly, which is not simply with textbooks, chalk and blackboard, but with multimedia, various apps and other tools. As to the network-based English phonetics teaching mode for English majors, the background of the subjects, the steps of the network-based teaching mode and the theoretical basis are introduced firstly. By selecting college English majors of 2017 in Anhui Institute of Information Technology as the subjects of the teaching experiment, the author collects and analyzes the data. It has been found that the students who have learned on the net have better English pronunciation than those learning in the traditional mode. Thus, the author suggests that teachers use the network-based teaching mode to improve the effectiveness of teaching and learning.

Keywords: Network-based teaching mode, phonetics, teaching experiment

D. Making a Reference List 制作参考文献列表

摘要完成后，参考文献看似换了任务，但仍服务同一个目标：让读者能够追溯摘要和正文中的来源。判断题先按课件答案复习，同时注意实际写作中应优先保证来源完整、权威且可检索。

Task Decide whether the following statements are true or false based on the passage above.

课件原答案标注为：PPT key: 1-F, 2-F, 3-T, 4-F, 5-T, 6-T。其中第 4、5 题与常见 reference-list 写作常识可能存在冲突；复习课件时按原答案记，实际写作时仍应让参考文献列表帮助读者定位来源，并优先选择权威且较新的资料。

1) You needn't list all your references at the end of the reference list.

False — 所有引用都必须列出。

2) The materials you have paraphrased can't be regarded as references.

False — 释义过的材料也算参考文献。

3) A reference can be used to avoid plagiarism.

True — 引用可避免抄袭。

4) A reference list can help readers to locate the materials that have been referred to.

课件原答案为 False。按一般写作规范，此句通常可理解为 True；这里保留课件答案并提示其疑点。

5) A good reference must be authoritative but not up-to-date.

课件原答案为 True。按一般写作规范，好的参考文献通常应同时考虑权威性和时效性；这里保留课件答案并提示其疑点。

6) IEEE style is widely used in computer science while MLA style is especially used in social sciences.

True — IEEE 多用于计算机科学，MLA 多用于社会科学/人文。
本单元收束：摘要是整篇论文的压缩版，关键词和参考文献则帮助论文被检索、被追溯。复习时按“摘要要素 → 动词时态 → 关键词类别 → 摘要流程 → 参考文献判断”的顺序检查。
