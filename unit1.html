UNIT 1

学术写作风格与正式度
Academic Style & Formality

学术写作的特征 · 正式用词 · 正式语法 · 文体辨识与改写

🎯 本单元学习目标

  • 理解学术语篇的风格与特征；
  • 区分学术体与个人化文体；
  • 识别不同的正式度层级；
  • 在学术写作中使用恰当的词句。
阅读路径：先建立学术写作的风格意识，再用选词、语法和衔接手段把口语化表达改成正式表达，最后通过文体辨识和段落改写，把规则用到真实句子里。

I. Warm-up 热身

Questions Start from the purpose and style of academic writing.

  1. How is academic writing different from other forms of writing?
  2. What is your purpose of initiating an academic research?
  3. What are the key characteristics of academic writing?
本单元的核心是"正式度（formality）"：学术写作要求客观、严谨、结构正式，并引用他人成果。

II. Sample Reading 范例阅读

学术写作的四大特征及其原因（高频考点，务必记牢）：
特征原因（英文原文）
Objectivity 客观性The readers need evidence-based facts rather than your personal opinions.
Complexity 复杂性Academic writing often discusses difficult, challenging ideas which can only be expressed with particular grammar and language.
Formal structure 正式结构Academic writing should be clear, concise and structured.
Referencing 引用Scientific discoveries are made by building on the ideas of other people. Another reason is to avoid plagiarism.

Task 1 Summarize the key characteristics of academic writing and explain why they prevail.

ReasonFeature
The readers need evidence-based facts rather than personal opinions.
Difficult ideas often require particular grammar and dense language.
The text needs to be clear, concise and structured.
Scientific discoveries build on other people's ideas and must avoid plagiarism.
这道题对应课件 Sample Reading 的第一项任务。记忆顺序可压缩为：facts → objectivity；difficult ideas → complexity；clear structure → formal structure；build on others → referencing。

Task 3 Which word is more suitable for an academic paper?

规律：学术写作偏好更正式、更精确的单词，避免口语化短语。

1. The government has made ___ progress in solving environmental problems.

considerable 比 good 更正式、更精确。

2. We ___ encouraging results.

obtain 是 get 的正式替换词。

3. The results of ___ different projects have been encouraging.

numerous 替换口语化的 a lot of；同句中 pretty good 也应改为 encouraging。

4. A loss of jobs is one of the ___ if the process is automated.

用名词 consequences 替换松散的从句 things that will happen。

5. The future of Federal funding is ___.

uncertain 替换习语 up in the air（悬而未决）。

III. Language Focus 语言重点

A. Word Choice 选词

学术文体的一个显著特征是选用更正式的词，尤其把"短语动词"换成"单个正式动词"。
非正式Researchers looked at the way strain builds up around a fault.
学术体Researchers observed the way strain accumulates around a fault.

Task 2 Rewrite with a single formal verb（开放题）

  1. The implementation of CIM has brought about some serious problems.
  2. The process should be done over until the desired results are achieved.
  3. Plans are being made to come up with a database for the region.
  4. Subtle changes in the earth's crust were picked up by these new devices.
  5. Proposals to construct new nuclear reactors have met with great resistance.
显示参考答案

  2. should be repeated until ...
  3. to devise / compile / create a database ...

B. Formal Grammar and Style 正式语法与文体

四条保持正式文体的规则（高频考点）：
  • 避免缩写： won't → will not
  • 使用正式否定式： not...any → no；not...much → little；not...many → few
  • 限制 run-on 表达：少用 and so forth / etc. → 用 "and other + 名词" 概括
  • 不直接称呼读者 you；少用直接疑问句。
非正式The analysis didn't yield any new results.
正式The analysis yielded no new results.
非正式You can see the results in Table 1.
正式The results can be seen in Table 1.
直接疑问What can be done to lower costs?
学术体We now need to consider what can be done to lower costs, or how costs may be lowered.

Task Rewrite to make them more formal（开放题）

  1. If you fail the exam, you can't enter the university.
  2. So, why did the bridge collapse? There're a lot of reasons.
  3. You can clearly see the difference between these two processes.
  4. These special tax laws have been enacted in six states: Iowa, Illinois, Ohio, etc.
  5. So far there hasn't been much research on how conflict influences the level of trust and respect in a group.
显示参考答案
  1. A passing grade on the exam is a prerequisite for admission to the university.
  2. There are many/several/various reasons for the collapse of the bridge.
  3. The difference between these two processes is clear.
  4. These special tax laws have been enacted in six states, including Iowa, Illinois and Ohio.
  5. There has been little research on how conflict influences the level of trust and respect in a group.

C. Linking Words and Flow 连接词与流畅性

短而零碎、衔接差的句子读起来很别扭。连接词能保持流畅、明确表达观点间的关系。意思同等重要的叫并列观点（coordinate ideas），可合并成并列句（compound sentence）。改写练习的要点：把零碎短句用连词整合。

Task Rewrite the following passage into a text with an easy flow.

A conjunction connects words, phrases, or clauses. It indicates the relationship between the elements. These elements connect. We often find the following thing. In only one single sentence a conjunction contains one or more ideas. These ideas may be equal or unequal in importance.

显示参考改写
A conjunction connects words, phrases, or clauses and can also indicate the relationship between the elements it connects. In only one single sentence we often find that it contains one or more ideas which may be equal or unequal in importance. When the ideas are equal, they are called coordinate ideas. These sentences may be joined into a compound sentence that shows the relationship between the two ideas.

IV. Academic Writing Skills 学术写作技能

A. Recognizing Different Styles 辨识不同文体

Task Where does each text come from?

1. "...infrared lasers are being used to capture live microbes. Working like optical tweezers, the pressure of the laser light traps minute objects..."

科技报道式语言，出自科学杂志（Science magazine）。

2. "All we ask is that you spend two hours of your time attending a Barratt Sales presentation. There, you'll discover the benefits of timeshare..."

直接称呼 you、推销口吻，是广告（Advertisement）。

3. "There were only two Americans stopping at the hotel. ... Their room was on the second floor facing the sea..."

简洁白描的叙事文体，海明威短篇《Cat in the Rain》。

4. "Although many studies have already been done, more studies are needed to determine the effects of microcomputer-assisted instruction..."

指出研究空白、用 more studies are needed，是研究论文（A research paper）。

5. "The parents of a seven-year-old Australian boy woke to find a giant python crushing and trying to swallow him..."

事件＋引语的新闻报道笔法，出自报纸（A newspaper）。

B. Choosing Appropriate Styles 选择恰当文体

学术文本通常精确、可靠、正式、有结构，并用证据支撑观点以避免歧义。

Task 1 Suppose you are going to write an essay for your teacher's assignment. Which of the following sentences would you like to use for your writing? Then give your reasons.（开放题）

显示参考答案

原课件 10 句的 Y/N 答案为：1-Y　2-Y　3-Y　4-Y　5-N　6-N　7-N　8-N　9-N　10-N。

判否（N）的理由分别为：口语表达（oral expressions）；用了第一人称单数、属口头表达；用了第一人称单数；句子松散（loosely-connected）；用了口语短语动词（verbal phrase）；来自报纸广告；结构过于复杂；呈现的是他人观点等。判 Y 的句子多采用被动语态、结构正式。

Task 2 Revise the paragraph into academic style（开放题）

Original (informal): Why are there so many jams on the roads these days? One thing is that public transportation like trains, buses, etc., is so dear. A long time ago cars cost a lot, but now they've got a lot cheaper. Another thing is that driving is a lot nicer than waiting for a bus. The trouble is that if everyone buys a car, the roads get packed.

显示参考答案

There appear to be two principal reasons for the growing traffic congestion. Firstly, public transportation has become increasingly expensive in relation to the falling cost of driving. In addition, car ownership is more convenient than using public transportation. These factors together result in higher vehicle density.

要点：口语词→正式词（jams→traffic congestion, dear→expensive, nicer→more convenient）；零碎句用 firstly / in addition 连接；去掉 etc. 和直接疑问句。
